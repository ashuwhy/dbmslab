## Roles: 

Student, Teacher, Analyst, Admin

1. Student:
- Apply for courses and topics
- View course materials, content, and assignments
- Submit assignments
- See grades 

2. Instructor:
- Create courses and topics ( to be verified by admin)
- Create and manage course content, and assignments
- Grade assignments
- Approve the student's course registration
- View student progress and performance

3. Analyst:
- Access and analyze student performance data
- Generate reports on student progress and course effectiveness
- Course registration and management data
- View courses and topics which are highly demanded by students
- View student demographics, category, and skill

4. Admin:
- Approve instructor registration 
- Approve course and topic creation by instructors
- Manage user accounts and permissions
- Approve analyst registration
- View all data and reports generated by analysts

# Registration and Approval Process:

1. Student 
- Students can register on the platform by providing necessary information such as name, email, age, skill_level, country, and password.
- After registration, student can opt for login with email and password
- Students can browse available courses and apply for the ones they are interested in.
- Student can view the course they are enrolled and the course materials, content, and assignments.
- The instructor will review the student's application and approve or reject it based on their criteria.

2. Instructor
- Instructors can register on the platform by providing necessary information such as name, email, teaching_years and  password.
- After registration, Instructors can login using email and password
- Instructors are approved by admin.
- Instructor create courses and topics, which will be subject to approval by the admin.
- Once approved, instructors can manage their courses, create and upload course materials, and assignments.
- Instructors can also view the students enrolled in their courses and grade their assignments.

3. Analyst
- Analysts can register on the platform by providing necessary information such as name, email and password.
- After registration, analysts can login using email and password
- Analysts are approved by admin.

4. Admin
- Admin approves instructor and analyst registration.
- Admin can create other admin accounts if necessary.
